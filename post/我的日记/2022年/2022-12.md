A:In French, the plural form of "bagage" is "bagages". To distinguish between singular and plural when translating back to English, you would use the words "baggage" (singular) and "baggages" (plural). 

Q:But "baggage" is uncountable Noun in english... 

A:Yes, "baggage" is an uncountable noun in English. However, when translating from French to English, the distinction between singular and plural is still important. For example, "j'ai un bagage" would be translated as "I have one piece of baggage" and "j'ai des bagages" would be translated as "I have multiple pieces of baggage".

### 碰撞安全的问题

车流瓶装的问题在于 step-length value="0.01" 默认太低的话会降低决策精度。

另外就是合理的车辆模型，默认 Krauss 就很好，如果选了SmartSK 一开始就会乱作一团。

在碰撞发生前将车辆停止一段固定时间（collision.stoptime），这就模拟了交通堵塞和交通混乱。

- **teleport**: (the default): the follower vehicle is moved (teleported) to the next edge on its route
- **warn**: a warning is issued
- **none**: no action is taken
- **remove**: both vehicles are removed from the simulation

```xml
<processing>
     <collision.action value="remove"/>
     <collision.stoptime value="100"/>
</processing>
```

### 科里化

原本的位置计算 `position - i * (DISTANCE+LENGTH)` 不能仅仅将distance 和length 变成变量，因为i* 将前面的结果重置了。

需要一个能记住前面结果的函数，在此基础上减去distance+length

```Python
def get_position(init_position):
    current = init_position
    def f(length):
        nonlocal current
        current -= length
        return current
    return f
……

get_insert_postion = get_position(position)

```
