# heterogeneity

* More on means, less on dispersion
  * $i^2$ =50% 不能区别坡度
  
* 置信区间不能反应分散的取向，而是准确度。
  * Predict interval: Mean $\pm$ 2x std Dev 是唯一能知道 effect vary 
  
* | Does effect vary at all?   | p value             | How much it cost -> more than 0 |
  | -------------------------- | ------------------- | ------------------------------- |
  | What proportion is real?   | $i^2$               | I will get 70% discount         |
  | How much does effect vary? | Prediction interval | how much will I pay             |

* Role of heterogeneity in meta-analysis:
  * Narrow case v.s Wide case
  * must study with means
