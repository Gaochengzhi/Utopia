## Conclusion 

卡车的影响

Trucks have a significant impact on the following characteristics. Whether as a leader or a follower, the following behavior of trucks tends to maintain a greater gap and a lower speed, that is, a larger time headway. Under the high-speed driving condition of 25–30 m/s, when the truck follows the car, the average time headway is 2–3 s, and the average gap is 70 m. Whereas the car follows the car, the average time headway is 1.5 s, and the average gap is 40 m.
